BA80: Location Prepositions
Used by the routine at YouSeeEntry.
These are written directly to $AFFD which updates the copy for: "You are XXXX[0x16]".

BA82 DEFB $7B,$83 "INSIDE"
BA84 DEFB $78,$83 "IN"
BA86 DEFB $AA,$84 "ON"
BA88 DEFB $70,$80 "AT"
